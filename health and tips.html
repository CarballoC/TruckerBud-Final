Health & Tips

All this recomendation is based on the personal opinion and personal experience of the creator.
Health is a serious topic. It is necessary to make an effort an focus on health.

When you are a truck driver is very important to monitor your health. It is hard to stay in shape. Specially those who drive long distance. There are many ways to train or excercise even if you are doing long hauls.
