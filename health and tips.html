Health & Tips

Health is a serious topic. It is necessary to make an effort an focus on health.

When you are a truck driver is very important to monitor your health. It is hard to stay in shape. Specially those who drive long distance. There are many ways to train or exercise even if you are doing long hauls.

Food and Workout

When you are a truck driver is very important to monitor your health. It is hard to stay in shape. Specially those who drive long distance. There are many ways to train or exercise even if you are doing long hauls.

All this recommendation is based on the personal opinion and personal experience of the creator.

Monday Routine

Breakfast

  • Bowl of oatmeal
  • Whole egg

Lunch

  • Chicken Breast
  • Veggies

Workout routine(before dinner)

  • Walk for 10-15 mins
  • 3 sets high knees
  • 3 sets jumping jacks
  • 3 sets jump squat
  • 3 sets body weight squat
  • 3 sets walking lunge

Dinner

  • Chicken breast or steak
  • Brown rice or Quinoa
  • Veggies
