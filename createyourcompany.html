Are you ready to start you own company?

In order to make your business official you can follow the following steps. These steps have the documentations amd permits you need to start

LLC stands for Limited Liability Company. The owner of this company can be call member. This company can has more than one member (multimember). The reason is Limited is because you are separating your personal assets from your business assets. If your company for any reason has any issue then your personal assets are untouchable. The cost of forming your LLC depends on what you choose and your State. The cost could be between $50-$500.

Those commercial vehicles used to transport passengers or haul cargo in interstate commerce they need an USDOT number. You will need to register your vehicle with the FMCSA. You can find all this information at FMCSA.dot.gov

MC Number, is required to have interstate Athority in Addition to a DOT number. You can find all this information at FMCSA.dot.gov

What is a BOC-3? This stands for "Blanket of Coverage". This is a form that you will need fill. Do not worried you do not have to do it by yourself. All the MC applicants are required to have a process agent on file with the FMCSA. This agent does take care of this form, so the legal documents can be served across state line. The cost for this could be between $30-$40.

You already know that any vehicles has to have insurance. In order to get your insurance you will have to make a down payment which is really based on a couple of aspects related to your license record. On average this down payment could be between $3000-$5000.

IRP stands for International Registration Plan. This is basicly the plate which has a cost around $1600. This last for one year after this period of time you will need to renovate it.

IFTA refers to International Fuel Tax Agreement. This agreement is between the 48 lower United States and the Canada provinces. Essentially this was created to simplify the reporting of fuel use for motor carrier that operate in more than one State or Canada province. The carrier receives an IFTA license and two decals (special sticker) for each vehicle.

UCR refers to Unified Carrier Registration. It is necessary the annual registration. The cost of the UCR is around $60.

The 2290 is the Heavy Highway Vehicle Use Tax and has a cost of $550. This payment is due to June every year. Lets say you bought a truck in the middel of the year. Well, your payment that year will be based on the date when you bought that truck.

If you equipment exceed 10,000 lbs you will need the DOT inspection. You can perform this inspection at almost any DOT Department. The cost of DOT inspection may vary depends on the State you perform it.

It is required by FMCSA and DOT to be enrolled in the Drug Program. Hit the link for more information

The decals have to be visible in your truck. These decals will contain the information of your truck and carrier. The price of these decals may vary based on the service or company that you decide to use to make them.
